# Research Findings

This file contains key findings from research that establish new baselines of understanding. These findings inform future research to determine if new discoveries represent genuine progress or already-known information.

## Purpose

When the researcher agent conducts investigations, the significant findings are:
1. Recorded in the Knowledge Graph as entities
2. Synced here by the KG Librarian
3. Used as baseline knowledge for future research

## Format

Each finding should include:
- **Date**: When the finding was established
- **Source**: Research brief or change report that produced it
- **Topic**: What area this covers
- **Key Insight**: The core discovery
- **Implications**: How this affects the project
